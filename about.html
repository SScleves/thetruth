About

I’m an engineer. I spend my days making systems observable — finding the signal that explains why something behaves the way it does. At some point I started applying the same habit to older questions, and noticed the pieces didn’t fit the story I’d been given.

This site is where I put the pattern down in writing: a book, and a slow series of discussions. I’m not asking anyone to believe anything. I’m asking whether the same shape keeps appearing — and why.
