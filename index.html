The pattern

▲The stones

Göbekli Tepe at 9600 BCE. Rain-worn erosion on the Sphinx. Precision that degrades instead of improving. A timeline that doesn’t add up.

Dozens of unconnected cultures remembering the same flood — and the same figure who brings knowledge, and is always punished for it.

▴The physics

Observation, entanglement, information at the bottom of everything. A system that looks, uncomfortably, like it’s describing itself.
